Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours at Little Learners Academy? +
Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We also offer extended care options for parents who need early drop-off or late pick-up.
Is there a uniform policy for students? +
Yes, students are required to wear a uniform. This helps promote equality and a sense of community.
What extracurricular activities are available for students? +
We offer sports, arts, music, and language clubs to enhance students’ learning experience.
How do you handle food allergies and dietary restrictions? +
We take food allergies seriously and work closely with parents to provide safe meal options.
What is the teacher-to-student ratio at Little Learners Academy? +
Our average ratio is 1 teacher for every 12 students to ensure personalized attention.
How do you handle discipline and behavior management? +
We focus on positive reinforcement, guidance, and open communication with parents.
How do I apply for admission to Little Learners Academy? +
Applications can be submitted online or at our front office. We also schedule tours for new parents.
Do you provide transportation for students? +
Yes, we provide safe and reliable bus services with designated pick-up and drop-off points.
